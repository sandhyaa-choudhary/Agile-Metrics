About DORA Metrics

DORA (DevOps Research and Assessment) metrics are based on six years of research and data from over 32,000 professionals worldwide. These four metrics have been proven to indicate software delivery performance and organizational effectiveness.

Teams that excel in these metrics ship features faster, have more stable systems, and respond to incidents more quickly. Use this assessment to identify where your team stands and what improvements could have the highest impact.
